Tranquility garden Rose

Tranquility is easily my favorite white garden rose in the garden. It’s a great rose for cut floral arranging. Tranquility (Ausnoble) is an English Shrub Rose Bred By David Austin and introduced in 2004.

Westminster Abbey Garden Roses

Upon arrival the blooms are tight, give them a fresh cut and into water to let them rehydrate. This is a hardy rose, the petals are firm and each bloom is packed full of petals. Give it some time to fully bloom open. The great news is it is a very long lasting bloom, as you can see here it looks fine on day 10 and it easily lasted 14 days.

Can you share about Chandlerie?

Through Lunaria, the past few years have allowed me to make friendships with so many in the Colorado market and beyond. I have partnered with Chandlerie for a handful of events, as well as used their candles in my weddings and styled shoots. I love the color palettes they offer and if they dont have a certain color you need, they can make it for you! On top of having amazing colors, they are sustainable – reusing wax waste from candles that would otherwise go into the trash. Love it!
